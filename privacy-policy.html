Privacy Policy for Blocalcs

Last updated: April 3, 2025

Blocalcs respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly. However, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect limited user data for ad personalization and performance.

Information Collection

Blocalcs does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not use analytics, create user accounts, or store any user data.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to serve in-app advertisements. Google may collect data such as device identifiers, interaction data, and approximate location to serve personalized ads.

For more details, see:

  • Google’s Advertising Privacy & Terms
  • Google AdMob & User Data

User Controls on iOS

You can control ad tracking and personalization through iOS settings:

  • Open Settings
  • Go to Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" to limit personalized advertising

Children’s Privacy

Blocalcs is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe your child has submitted personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date.
